About Me

Hello! I’m Rohan Erasala, an undergraduate student at the University of Michigan. I’m a designer, developer, engineer, and so much more! On my website, you can get a little bit of a glimpse of what makes me, me!


My Story:

After graduating from Northville High School in 2019, I moved onto the University of Michigan to study computer science.

It was at the University of Michigan that I developed a strong interest in computers, applications, websites, and how they are designed and how they operate. After working at companies such as Auria, Quicken Loans, and Ford, I have developed the ability to not just design great looking solutions, but the necessary technical and programming skills to make my prototypes and wireframes into a reality. I am currently seeking internship opportunities for Summer 2022. If you are interested in learning more about what I can do, please use the contact form or reach out to me by email (rerasala@umich.edu)!
